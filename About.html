A&A Laris Resources

A&A Laris Resources is a registered restaurant business established in 2023 under the Companies Act 1965. We operate Restoran Ayam Penyet Crispy, strategically located opposite Hospital Machang, Kelantan. In addition to our restaurant services, A&A Laris Resources also offers catering services for various special occasions, including weddings, dinner events, birthday celebrations, and other private or corporate functions.
